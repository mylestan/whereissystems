Welcome to Where Is Systems :)

We're trying to understand what Systems Design Engineering is all about. This is an attempt to visualize the movement of Systems Design Engineering talent since hte program's creation in 1968. By examining the shift in dimensions such as industry and location over time, we hope to understand how the demand for the 'Systems mindset' has developed and changed over time, and the impact that graduates have had on the world
